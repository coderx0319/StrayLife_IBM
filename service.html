Welcome to our StrayLife service! We understand that your furry companions deserve the best care and attention, which is why we offer a range of top-notch services tailored to meet their needs. Whether you're a busy or simply want to provide them with extra pampering, our dedicated team is here to ensure their happiness and well-being.

Veterinary

Facilities for day-to-day treatment, immunization, vaccination, treatment etc. of pets, small animals and bird.
